About This Dashboard

This visualization explores the hidden structure of college fight songs using data from FiveThirtyEight.

  • Lyrical Ingredients: An UpSet plot showing every unique combination of tropes (words like "Fight", "Victory", "Rah") used in the songs.
  • Trope Network: A graph where schools are connected if their lyrics are >75% similar. Nodes cluster by content, not conference.
  • Song Topology: A map of song speed (BPM) vs length. The size of the bubble represents how many times they yell "Fight!".
  • Origin Story: Shows how the songs were born—whether by students, contests, or official decree.
