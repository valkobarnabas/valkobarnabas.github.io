About This Dashboard

Explore the hidden structure of college fight songs.

  • Lyrical Ingredients: Which clichés (Tropes) appear in the lyrics?
  • Trope Network: Schools grouped by conference. Node color = Trope Count.
  • Song Topology: Speed (BPM) vs Length. Color intensity = Number of "Fights" in lyrics.
  • Origin Story: How the song was created.
